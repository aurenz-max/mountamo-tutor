## Current State

No content has been authored for Grade 5. No CSV files, no subskills, no prerequisite graph.

**Important context:** Grade 5 is the capstone of elementary school. It consolidates all elementary mathematics and prepares students for the abstraction of middle school. Math introduces volume, coordinate planes, and full fraction operations. ELA moves toward literary analysis and argument. This grade must ensure readiness for the Grade 6 transition to ratios, expressions/equations, and disciplinary science models.

### From Grade 4

- **OPS004** (multi-step problems, patterns with rules) feeds into **OPS005** (numerical expressions with parentheses, order of operations, generating and comparing patterns)
- **NBT004** (multi-digit arithmetic, place value to 1,000,000) feeds into **NBT005** (place value for decimals, multi-digit multiplication with standard algorithm, decimal operations)
- **NF002** (equivalent fractions, add/subtract with like denominators, multiply fraction by whole number, decimals to hundredths) feeds into **NF003** (add/subtract unlike denominators, multiply fractions, divide unit fractions, fractions as division)
- **MEAS004** (unit conversions, area/perimeter, angle measurement, line plots) feeds into **MEAS005** (unit conversions within systems, volume, line plots with fractions)
- **GEOM004** (points/lines/rays/angles, classify triangles, symmetry) feeds into **GEOM005** (coordinate plane, classify 2D figures by properties, shape hierarchy)
- **LA001** and **LA002** (Grade 4 theme, text structure, compare accounts) feed into **LA001** and **LA002** (Grade 5 theme across texts, multiple main ideas, analyze multiple accounts)

- **OPS005** (expressions with parentheses, order of operations) feeds into **EE001** (write/evaluate expressions with exponents, equivalent expressions, solve equations)
- **NBT005** (decimal operations, multi-digit multiplication/division) feeds into **NS001** (multi-digit decimal operations, GCF/LCM) and **RP001** (unit rates, percent)
- **NF003** (fraction operations, fractions as division) feeds into **NS001** (divide fractions, negative numbers) and **RP001** (ratios, proportional relationships)
- **MEAS005** (volume, unit conversions) feeds into **GEOM006** (volume with fractions, surface area)
- **GEOM005** (coordinate plane first quadrant, shape classification) feeds into **GEOM006** (coordinate geometry) and **NS001** (coordinate plane all quadrants)

## Authoring Notes

1. **Fraction operations mastery:** NF003 is the culmination of the elementary fraction sequence. Students must add/subtract with unlike denominators (requiring equivalent fractions from NF002), multiply fractions and mixed numbers, and divide with unit fractions. The subskill progression should ensure each operation is grounded in visual models before transitioning to algorithms.

2. **Volume as a new concept:** MEAS005 introduces three-dimensional measurement through volume. This is the first time students work with cubic units. Scaffold from counting unit cubes to using the formula V = l x w x h to composing volumes of composite figures (additive volume).

3. **Coordinate plane introduction:** GEOM005 introduces the coordinate plane (first quadrant only). This is foundational for all middle school graphing. Ensure subskills cover ordered pair notation, plotting points, and interpreting coordinates in real-world contexts (maps, distance).

4. **Order of operations:** OPS005 introduces PEMDAS with parentheses, brackets, and braces. This is a critical prerequisite for Grade 6 expressions and equations. Common misconceptions (left-to-right for multiplication/division) should be explicitly addressed in subskills.

5. **Middle school readiness:** Every unit should be designed with the Grade 5-to-6 transition in mind. Students who complete Grade 5 must be ready for ratios (RP001), negative numbers (NS001), and formal expressions/equations (EE001). Gaps at this stage compound rapidly in middle school.

6. **Standards alignment:** Mathematics aligns to Common Core Grade 5 (final elementary year). Science aligns to NGSS Grade 5 and 3-5 Engineering Design. ELA aligns to Common Core Grade 5. The 3-5 Engineering Design band concludes at this grade.
