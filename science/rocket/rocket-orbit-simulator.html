Orbital Mechanics

This simulator models rocket launches and orbital mechanics using these key equations:

F = m × a
Δv = v_e × ln(m_0 / m_f)
v_orbit = √(G × M / r)
T = 2π × √(r³ / (G × M))

Where:

  • F = force (thrust - drag)
  • m = current mass
  • a = acceleration
  • Δv = change in velocity (delta-v)
  • v_e = exhaust velocity
  • m_0 = initial mass
  • m_f = final mass
  • v_orbit = orbital velocity
  • G = gravitational constant
  • M = mass of Earth
  • r = orbital radius
  • T = orbital period
